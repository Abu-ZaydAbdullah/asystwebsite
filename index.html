What is Asyst?

Asyst is the crossing paths of crowd sourcing and helping the elderly. With Asyst, an elderly person in need of aid (tech help, grocery shopping, a ride to a doctor's appointment, etc.) can request for help. The people who are giving the help are not employees of Asyst, but simply people in the community wanting to help out. The elderly person will directly pay the person who did the job, and Asyst will take 15 percent. In addition the app, there is also a Voicemail interface in which an elderly person can call in for help, as most of them won't be tech-savvy and may not have smartphones at all.

 

Asyst allows for more people to be helped, because we don't have actual employees with salaries. People pick their own hours, what jobs they want to help with, and how much money they want to make. The elderly will receive aid with the things they can't do on their own, not have to spend thousands of dollars living with someone to help them, and can choose when they want help on any given day.
